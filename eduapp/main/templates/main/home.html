Welcome to edubook

Our programs focus on developing the entire education technology and innovation sector in information technology.

Edubook is India's education technology and innovation industry hub. Through connection and collaboration, we accelerate India's EdTech ecosystem globally.

We connect a community of education providers, industry participants, and EdTech entrepreneurs committed to reimagining learning in the digital age.

As education transitions to borderless digital delivery, our diverse ecosystem will impact the future of learning globally from India.

We have a range of services supporting EdTech companies at each stage of their journey, whilst also connecting education providers and industry participants into the broader ecosystem.
